History of Egypt

Ancient Egypt was one of the world's earliest civilizations, flourishing along the Nile River. It spanned over 3,000 years and left behind remarkable achievements in art, architecture, and governance.

Monuments & Architecture

The Great Pyramids of Giza, the Sphinx, and Karnak Temple are iconic symbols of ancient Egyptian ingenuity and craftsmanship.

Artifacts & Relics

Discover treasures like the Rosetta Stone, Tutankhamun's Mask, and the Book of the Dead, which offer insights into ancient Egyptian life.

Culture & Religion

Egyptians worshipped gods like Ra, Osiris, and Isis. Their daily lives were deeply influenced by religion, agriculture, and the Nile River.
